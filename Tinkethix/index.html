TEAM TINK ETHIX

Tink Ethix is a advanced robotics team Of Seth Anandram jaipuria School with extended history of Atal Tinkering Lab. It provides a platform with huge exposure in the field to students and aims at inculcating advanced engineering and automation skills.

1
“ FIX THE CAUSE NOT THE SYMPTOM ”

We use our problem-solving capabilities to the fullest and always aim for a brighter future.

2
“ KNOWLEDGE IS POWER ”

We aim to inculcate and foster Technological and Tinkering skills into the young minds of tomorrow.

3
“ TECHNOLOGY IS BEST WHEN IT BRINGS PEOPLE TOGETHER ”

We are extremely enthusiastic and determined when it comes to creation of new technologies and interfaces that provide meaningful and enjoyable experiences.
